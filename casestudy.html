Overview

The community website redesign project aims to modernize and improve the existing website to create a more user-friendly experience for the community. The new website will be designed to serve as a comprehensive source of information for local residents, businesses, visitors and volunteers.

Insight

Users are unable to navigate swiftly through pages due to the clutter of information and images and UI patterns that are not accessible therefore causing confusion and frustration.

Problem

Adults who are new to a community need to feel more confident that they can access content like training, education, local events and activities because this will help them get more involved in their communities.

solution

Simplify the navigation and user flow to enable users to access comprehensive source of information about community activities, educational programmes and more.

RESEARCH GOALS

To better understand the user needs for easy user-friendly experience and navigation for the community, i and my team had to conduct a user research to help us understand usability pain points of the website and also usability problems for the audience. In order to achieve this we conducted an interview and survey questions for users and stakeholders of the community foundation website
